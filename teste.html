Real Weddings: An Intimate Tuscan Villa Celebration

When fashion designer Alessandra and art curator Luca decided to tie the knot, they envisioned a celebration that blended Italian tradition with contemporary elegance. Their three-day wedding in the rolling hills of Tuscany became the talk of the season.

From the hand-painted invitations to the locally-sourced five-course meal, every detail reflected their shared love of art and sustainability. The bride's custom-made gown, featuring fabric woven from recycled materials, set a new standard for eco-conscious bridal fashion.
